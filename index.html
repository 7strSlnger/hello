Who We Are

We're Amy & Scott

Two Gen X best friends who grew up on MTV, mix tapes, and the smell of a Blockbuster on a Friday night. We started GenX Besties because we noticed nobody was keeping it real for our generation the way we wanted.

No filters, no fluff — just two besties talking about the things that matter to us: the music that defined us, the movies we still quote, the world we're navigating, and everything in between.

If you grew up skeptical, independent, and a little bit loud — you found your people.
